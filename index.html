Welcome to Paket Mutfak

We build systems that make good food accessible.

Our end-to-end approach to operations & technology.

Order & Payment Compilers

Consolidates orders into a single interface. Accept all payment methods.

Map Compiler

Simplifies order bundling by a mapping directory, consolidating address formats.

SUFLE App

Manages lifecycle of raw materials. Tracks and communicates directly with our ERP.

PM Courier App (PMC)

Enables couriers to self-assign packages, relays real-time delivery status updates.

We centered our technology around facilitating seamless flow of information.

For this we need to overcome 3 main challenges:

1. Multiple storefronts on different platforms create information blindness.

2. Oversight of multiple moving parts is required to maintain a healthy operation.

3. Dynamic decision making is needed to keep up with real-time consumption.
